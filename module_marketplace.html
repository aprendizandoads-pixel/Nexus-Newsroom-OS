How to Install

  1. Download the .zip package of the desired module.
  2. Navigate to the Modules & Admin section in your Nexus OS dashboard.
  3. Click the Upload Package (.zip) button in the top right.
  4. Select the file. The system will verify the signature and manifest.
  5. Once installed, click the toggle to activate the module.
